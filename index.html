Hey, my name is Apollo Uitto!

I am a 20 year old aspiring programmer from Helsinki, Finland. My main focus of programming centers around web development and video games. I also like to do other projects, such as programs about calculating and generating. My main programming languages used are HTML, CSS, Javascript and C#. As for the use of AI, I occasionally use it to speed up the process, but I always carefully examine the code to test it indeed works as intended.

Here are my primary skills listed down below

Game Development (Godot + C#)

For the longest time, I have had the dream of creating a game. In 2022, I finally decided to take real steps towards the goal as I began learning Unity and the code it operatres on, C#. After complications with the company back in 2023, I decided to move on to Godot, which also supported C#, the language I had grown fond of. Since then, I have been learning and experimenting inside Godot, creating many different kinds of games, some bigger, some smaller, though none yet released. Through all of my ideas, I have settled on two projects, which are my current focuses and the games I truly plan on one day publishing.

Web Developmemnt (HTML, CSS, Javascript)

I initially learned web development around the time I started coding in Unity, after taking a class where we created a webgame. Since then, I have worked on several websites, including business, tournament and personal blog sites. I have not yet published a website of my own as there have been no need, but I have been thinking of starting a blog for my game development, in which I would like to put my web dev skills to the absolute test with all the cool features I can think of.

Good Work Ethic

I tend to have a lot of energy and motivation, which ofcourse naturally helps me be more productive and efficient in my work. I also get to the flow state quite easily and can work long times, completely focused on the task ahead.

I always set clear goals in my mind, which I divide into macro and micro goals. Macro goals are usually big milestones or even the end state, while micro goals are much smaller and usually achieved within 15 to 60 minutes. This helps in maintaining a consistent pace in my work, as well as having a clear vision of the project at all times.

Statistics

I am not a super mathematical person, however, different statistics and archives have always fascinated me for some reason. I always like to look and analyze statistics in different fields, and I also like to include them in my work in any way I can see fit.

This is not only really enjoyable for me, but also very useful in many cases, as statistics often reveal a lot, though you should never make direct assumptions solely based on stats.

Creativity and building

This is not limited to programming, although very much connected to it. I tend to get a lot of different ideas and solutions for problems, in all areas of life. Whether it's creative programming, level design or story telling, I like to "overthink" things and come up with many different approaches, then choosing the best one.

I always approach my work with efficiency and flexibility in mind. For example, I want my code to be as general as reasonably possible. Also, I want it to be as simple and lightweight as possible.
